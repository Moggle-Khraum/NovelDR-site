Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 26, 2026

NovelDR is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. Information We Collect

None. NovelDR does not collect any personal data, analytics, or usage statistics. The app works entirely offline.

2. Data Storage

All downloaded novels, reading progress, and settings are stored locally on your device only. We have no access to this data.

3. Permissions

NovelDR may request storage permission only to save downloaded novels to your device. No other permissions are required.

4. Third-Party Services

NovelDR does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, or advertising services.

5. Contact

If you have privacy concerns, please contact us via GitHub.
