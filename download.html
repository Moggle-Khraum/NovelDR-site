✦ Powerful Features

  • Supports multiple sites: ReadNovelFull, NovelBin, NovelFull, FreeWebNovel, LightNovelWorld, NovelFull.com, AllNovel.org, and Novgo.net
  • Reading Comfort: Adjustable Font size, Line spacing, Auto-scroll, Progress Tracker, & TTS-supported
  • Novel Export: Export novels into TXT, RTF, DOCX, PDF Letter, EPUB, and Kindle MOBI format.
  • Multiple App Themes: Dark, Light, Sepia, AMOLED, Warm, & Slate.
  • Data Control: Full Backup w/ Restore & Import File, & Bug Report.
  • Stay Updated: Novel updates in just 2 taps: Select Novel, and tap Update.
